Chicken Salad Recipe

Ingredients:
3 lbs raw chicken breast
1 large carrot
2 celery stalks
1 small onion
5 cups chicken broth
2 tbsp parsley
1.5 tsp tarragon
2 tsp lemon juice
1 tsp dijon mustard
2 tsp salt
1 tsp pepper

1. Chop carrot, 1 celery stalk, and onion into small pieces
2. Add chopped vegetables, 1.5 tbsp parsley and broth in medium saucepan, bring to boil
3. Add chicken breasts, return to boil, then turn to low heat for 20 minutes
4. Mix wet ingredients and herbs/spices in a large mixing bowl
5. Once chicken is done poaching, cut into small cubes
6. Mix chicken and wet ingredients in the bowl until all the cubes are coated evenly
